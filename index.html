ABOUT US

A year's worth of excitement, fervour and hard work packed into 3 amazing days. Felicity is the annual techno-cultural fest of IIIT-Hyderabad, and would go on from January 27th to 29th. This time, our theme celebrates that one merry period of life, when we were young, curious and free. This Felicity we dedicate these three days to walk you through your childhood once again, and take a dip in the nostalgia of the 90's. Felicity '17 - Forever Young.
